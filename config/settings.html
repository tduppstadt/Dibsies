To use a custom Google Web Fonts, go to the Google Web Fonts website and pick your font. On the instructions section it will show you the address of the font, e.g 'http://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Averia+Libre:700' - just enter the font part 'Averia+Libre:700' here and the template will do the rest.

Products

There are two options for showing related products:

  • Same type: Picks some other products from the same product type.
  • Related tags: Group your products using tags of the form 'related-group1'. E.g. Assign three products the tag 'related-bag' & when you view one item, the other two will show as related products.

Slides

  • If you want to use the text overlay, enter the text in the following format, separated by the 'pipe' character:
    Title | Main text | Price text | Button text
    Example:
    Glorious Eggs|These eggs were laid within three weeks of the winter solstice|£14.99|More Details
    If you do not want an item to show (i.e. the price) then leave it empty.
  • Make sure URLs begin with / or http:// or https://
  • To make use of alt text (for SEO) or title text (pops up when you hover your mouse over the slide), enter this in the URL field using the pipe character like this: "http://www.shopify.com|alt-text|title-text"
  • Suggested size for all slide images: 940x400
